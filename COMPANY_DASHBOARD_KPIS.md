# HygieneOps Innovator Founder Demo Narrative

## Product positioning

HygieneOps is a modular facilities intelligence SaaS platform by AlphaBridge SmartOps Ltd. It is designed for hygiene and cleaning operators that still depend heavily on WhatsApp, spreadsheets, paper checklists and manual supervision.

The platform connects the full hygiene operations lifecycle:

1. Client request and CRM intake.
2. LPO and contract workspace.
3. Site inspection and readiness.
4. Workforce compliance.
5. Shift allocation and scheduling.
6. Time, attendance and proof of presence.
7. Task execution and proof of service.
8. SiteComms group communication.
9. Finance, payroll and KPI reporting.
10. Data migration from previous software or spreadsheet systems.

## Innovation argument

HygieneOps is not positioned as a simple cleaning company website. It is a connected operating system for hygiene service delivery. The innovation is the combination of workforce compliance, site-readiness validation, proof-of-presence, proof-of-service, client sign-off, communications and finance intelligence in one workflow.

## Viability argument

The MVP can start with a validation pilot for hygiene companies, universities, offices and production factories. The first version collects enquiries, demonstrates dashboards, supports CSV migration, manages site inspection templates and shows how service can be converted into auditable evidence.

## Scalability argument

The system is built as a multi-tenant SaaS model, meaning each hygiene operator can have its own company account, users, clients, sites, workers, shifts, documents and reports. This supports regional, national and future international growth.

## Demonstration flow

1. Open the public HygieneOps site.
2. Explain the problem: fragmented hygiene operations and weak evidence of service delivery.
3. Show the modular product architecture.
4. Show the onboarding workflow.
5. Show the company dashboard.
6. Show CRM, contracts, inspections, workforce compliance and attendance modules.
7. Show the data migration/import pathway.
8. Explain the Supabase backend and tenant-isolated data model.
9. Explain security controls and UK GDPR readiness.
10. Close with pilot strategy and expansion plan.
