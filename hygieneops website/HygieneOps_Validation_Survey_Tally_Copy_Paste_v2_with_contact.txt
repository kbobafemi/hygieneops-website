# HygieneOps Validation Survey
A short validation survey for UK cleaning, hygiene and facilities service providers. Estimated completion time: 5-7 minutes.

**Direct contact**
Email: info@hygieneops.co.uk
Phone: +44 7443 365555
Website: hygieneops.co.uk

**Purpose**
We are validating HygieneOps before full development. Your answers will help us understand whether cleaning, hygiene and facilities providers need this workflow solution and what should be included in the first MVP.

**Concept summary**
HygieneOps is a proposed operations platform from AlphaBridge SmartOps Ltd for cleaning, hygiene and facilities service providers. The aim is to help teams manage client requests/LPOs, inspections, contract activation, workforce compliance, assignment deployment, evidence of work, and job-level financial visibility in one joined-up workflow.
