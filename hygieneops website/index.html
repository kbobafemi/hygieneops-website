About the company

AlphaBridge SmartOps Ltd

AlphaBridge SmartOps Ltd is a technology company building practical digital infrastructure for facilities management, hygiene operations, workforce compliance and operational intelligence. The company’s flagship product, HygieneOps, is designed to address the fragmented way cleaning and hygiene services are currently managed across WhatsApp messages, manual spreadsheets, disconnected payroll records, separate client instructions and limited real-time visibility.

The platform

What HygieneOps is designed to solve

Disconnected communication

Hygiene teams, supervisors, admin staff and client representatives often communicate across informal channels. HygieneOps introduces structured site-based group chat and operational records.

Manual operational processes

Client requests, LPOs, inspections, shift planning, compliance checks and service delivery can be managed in one organised workflow.

Limited visibility

HygieneOps aims to provide clear visibility across job status, staff readiness, compliance documents, client instructions, billing records and performance reporting.

Core modules

Key features planned for HygieneOps

01

Client Request & LPO Management

Capture service requests, client instructions, agreed rates, site requirements, worker quantity, shift times and contract terms.

02

Pre-Engagement Site Inspection

Record site condition, hygiene requirements, risk factors, materials needed, PPE responsibility and service readiness.

03

Workforce Compliance

Support right-to-work records, onboarding information, DBS checks, training documents and hygiene partner availability.

04

Shift Allocation & Job Execution

Assign hygiene professionals to client sites, track attendance, monitor job status and support supervisor oversight.

05

Site-Based Group Chat

Create structured communication channels for deployed hygiene staff, admin teams, supervisors and client-appointed representatives.

06

Financial Intelligence

Build visibility between client billing, worker pay, margin control, VAT records and management reporting.
